BoozyDev

Welcome to BoozyDev, a web development project by a computer science student who enjoys coding and with an occasional drink. This platform is a simple space for diving into the basics of back-end web development. Built with a mix of tech know-how and a laid-back vibe, BoozyDev is where programming meets crafting smooth online experiences. Whether you're a pro or just starting out, BoozyDev welcomes you to explore the blend of tech and a bit of inspiration.

About me

Welcome to BoozyDev! I've long been captivated by the world of back-end development. While front-end has its charms, back-end has always caught had my eyes. Thus, BoozyDev was born: a digital playground tailored for coding sessions paired with a cold one. After all, around here, we make frequent use of homeBrew. Join me in the exploration of back-end development with a refreshing twist at BoozyDev!

Link Creator

In my teenage years, I often relied on tools to generate short and memorable links. Recently, feeling nostalgic, I decided to try my hand at it. While my proficiency in nginx is a work in progress, I haven't quite mastered the art of managing redirections and proxies yet, resulting in somewhat lengthy links. Despite this, I'm proud of the outcome. Feel free to check out the results and join me on this journey of exploration!
